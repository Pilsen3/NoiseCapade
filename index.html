NOISE-CAPADE

Noise-capade is an experimental gallery featuring eight (8) abstract cymatic paintings created by the noise pollution recorded from various Metro Manila cities.

About

Project Noise-capade is an art gallery which consists of eight (8) paintings, with each painting representing a city and landmark in Central Metro Manila. The cities represented are Quezon City, Manila City, Mandaluyong City, Pasig City, Makati City, Pasay City, Taguig City, and Paranaque City. A collection of mixed recordings of industrial and environmental noises in Metro Manila is used to create abstract representational paintings. Each of the noise tracks and paintings falls under the Abstract Expressionism genre, with the visual outputs having their corresponding noise art. The artist’s goal is to capture the chaos that has been rapidly increasing as an effect of industrial development – as well as the current pandemic recovery phase of the country.

The cities and locations are North Avenue, Quezon City; Divisoria, Manila City; Boni Area, Mandaluyong City; Mega Market, Pasig City; Asia Pacific College, Makati City; Barangay Villamor, Pasay City; Bayani Road, Taguig City; DOST Overpass, Paranaque City.
